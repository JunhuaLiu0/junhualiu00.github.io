I am a second-year B.S. student at The Chinese University of Hongkong, Shenzhen
Currently, I am lucky to be a research assistant in Future Network of Intelligence Institute supervised by Prof. Fangxin Wang and doing research intern in SenseTime. Besides, I am also lucky to work with Prof. Huazhe Xu, Prof. Yan Wang.

My interest lies at the intersection of computer network, omnidirectional vision, and robot learning.
